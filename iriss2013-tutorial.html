IRiSS Computational Social Science workshop materials

Published: 二 15 四月 2014
By Rebecca Weiss

In updates.

tags: pelican publishing workshops

The Institute of Research in Social Science at Stanford has been offering a Computational Social Science short course for the past few summers.

Last year, I gave a workshop introducing the shell and basic text analysis in Python.

Slides and instructional materials are all located here: http://rjweiss.github.io/text-iriss2013/

I will probably be revising those materials into a more general set of text tutorials at some point.

Proudly powered by Pelican, which takes great advantage of Python.

The theme is by Smashing Magazine, thanks!
